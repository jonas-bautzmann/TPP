Installation Guide

Drag & Drop into your bookmark list

Hey,
this small plugin was written to make your Trustpilot workflow a bit nicer.
First you need to 'install' the script. Just drag & drop the image into your bookmark bar.
To start the plugin go to the Trustpilot reviews page and click on the created bookmark.

A small puzzle icon will appear a on the right side. That indicates that the plugin is loaded.
When you click on the icon you will get some extra actions which you can use as you like.

Beside that the core feature of this plugin is the easy way to tag reviews.
Just click on a review card and you will see all possible tags which you can use.
